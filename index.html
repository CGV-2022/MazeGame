WELCOME TO THE MAZE WALKER

We've heard you're on a journey to become a great warrior.

You've been tasked with the mission to complete these challenging maze runs in order to become one of the greatest.

Each level you will be transported to a unique maze. In order to win each level, you'll need to find your way around the maze to the enemy and defeat the enemy before the timer runs out.

Good luck!
